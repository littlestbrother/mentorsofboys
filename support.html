Support

Mentors of Boys is completely funded by the community. So we rely on people like you to help us mentor. Anything helps and everything is greatly appreciated; every dollar goes directly to benefit our boys. We have no paid staff and our administration costs are low. There are several ways in which you can help. You may choose to donate once a month, once a year, or once a lifetime. Whatever you choose is completely up to you.
